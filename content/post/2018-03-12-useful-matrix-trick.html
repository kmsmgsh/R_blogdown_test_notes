This is found by Chapter “the multivariate Normal distribution”" by T.W. Anderson(1984),“An Introduction to Multivariate Analysis”.P18 (31). The \[ \begin{aligned} &E(DZE+F)\\ &=(\sum _{h,g} d_{ih}Z_{hg}e_{gj}+f_{ij})=\sum_{h,g}d_{ih}(EZ_{h,g})e_{gj}+f_{ij} \end{aligned} \] It may useful to mark \(DZE\) is \(\sum _{h,g} d_{ih}Z_{hg}e_{gj}\), maybe can make quadratic form more handleable.
